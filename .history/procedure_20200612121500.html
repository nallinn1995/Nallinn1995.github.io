OUR PROCEDURE

01

Planning and Engineering

We first plan our project and segregate into several steps to handover to respective team.

We convey our client’s need to our manufacturing team very clear and customize our products accordingly

02

Estimation

We try to reduce our estimated value as much as possible ( not below the quality limit) to offer client a best price.

We estimate our project value and then it goes to managing authority for approval.

We always keep quality consideration first and then price.

03

Scheduling

Scheduling is the step where we categories and focus on each activity which we are going to execute.

We then forecast our deadline so as to achieve target with quality.

We also take buffer time into our schedule to face unexpected activities which cause delay.

04

Cash Flow Plan

Our specialized commercial and execution team make cash flow plan together so as to ease our activities to achieve targeted goal.

05

Site Inspection

We do site inspection before execution to assure our plan in practical execution.

06

Execution and Progress Monitoring

While execution of the project , each and every activity is monitored weekly by our higher management to ensure that we are on right track. At every stage , client’s exact need is been identified by our project manager and try to fulfill those without sacrificing our quality standards.

Finally we complete the job which complies QC of client as well as JS
